What is SWISTO?

SWISTO is Kenya's premier emergency procurement platform specifically designed for the hospitality industry. We bridge the gap between hotels in urgent need and verified suppliers, ensuring same-day delivery of essential supplies.

Our Mission

To eliminate operational disruptions in hospitality through reliable, tech-enabled emergency supply chain solutions.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's most trusted emergency supply network for the hospitality industry.

How We Serve the Hospitality Industry

For Hotels & Resorts

Instant access to verified suppliers, real-time stock visibility, and guaranteed same-day delivery for emergency needs. Protect your guest experience and operational continuity.
